About Me

My name is Oliver Heisel and I am an independant software developer and designer, along with being a hobby photographer. I am attending University of Minnesota - Duluth, studying Computer Science.

My main project right now is DisplayPlus, a third-party app for Even Realities AR Glasses. This is a native SwiftUI app that provides a unique, customizable HUD for the glasses, along with additional features like music playback with Apple Music and iOS Shortcuts support
